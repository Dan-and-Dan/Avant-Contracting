About Avant Contracting Group

Avant Contracting Group, LLC is a contracting based in upstate New York and seeks to provide clients with the best service possible. Avant is owned and managed by Dillon Bolognino, who is always onsite to make sure the work is done in a timely and efficient manner. We are a lead-safe certified firm, and are fully insured (as safety is our numnber one priority).

The services we provide range from home improvement tasks such as bathroom remodeling and dry wall to framing houses and everything in between. We are willing to take on jobs anywhere in the Northeast. We use only the highest quality of materials and take great pride in our work.

We take on any client (big or small) seeking skilled contractors to fulfill their construction needs. Avant works closely with our clients and we promise to work until the job is done. Our mission is to provide high quality work and to complete the task in a reasonable amount of time.
